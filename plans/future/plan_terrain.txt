Every location has a property "altitude". It can be from 0 to MAX_ALTITUDE.

If a location has an altitude of 1, display the floor block 2 times higher than it is, but with it's bottom level with the bottom's of floor blocks with altitude 0.
Altitude 2 = 3 times bigger. Altitude 3 = 4 times bigger, etc.

If a building is placed on a location with an altitude greater than 0, apply the altitude when drawing the building.
